# E-Commerce Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales and profit data to understand business performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, and profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

## Tools Used

* SQL
* Excel
* Power BI

## Key Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Track monthly and yearly sales trends
* Understand regional sales distribution
* Evaluate profit margins across different product categories

## Key Insights

* Total sales and total profit performance
* Category-wise sales and profit analysis
* Top-selling products by revenue
* Profit contribution by product category
* Regional sales performance

## Dashboard Features

* Total Sales and Profit Overview
* Category Performance Analysis
* Top Products by Sales
* Profit Distribution by Category
* Sales Trend Analysis

## Outcome

The dashboard helps businesses understand sales performance, identify profitable product categories, and make strategic decisions to improve revenue and profitability.
